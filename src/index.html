About me

I am a firefighter who is passionate about helping others and making a positive impact. However, constantly fighting fires can cause me to lose my passion, so I started exploring new challenges. This led me to web development, which has given me the same sense of urgency and excitement as responding to emergencies. Every web development project I work on feels like fighting a fire, and I love the rush of identifying and solving problems, seeing a project come together, and delivering a product that meets my clients' needs. Overall, I am always looking for ways to use my skills and passion to make a positive impact in the world, whether it's fighting fires, making deliveries, or building websites.
